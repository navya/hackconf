    • Mailing lists
    • IRC
    • Twitter
    • Google+
    • Facebook

  • I am an outstation participant. Will I be provided accommodation ?

    We’ll provide pointers for accomodation nearby.

    We are a group of F/OSS enthusiasts. You can look us on github.
